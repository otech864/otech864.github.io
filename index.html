Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter is a multiplayer FPS game where you engage in critical battles against other players in various game modes. Play in a quick battle to hop into the action immediately!

How to Play

Immerse yourself in battle

Skilled FPS players will love Hazmob FPS: Online Shooter. The game features excellent, clean 3D graphics with a fluid and intuitive control system that allows you to maneuver quickly and tactically. The maps are designed for fast-paced battles in a close-quarters setting, leaving it down to pure reflex and skill to get the job done.

Upgrade your weapons

The weapon collection is extensive, with 55+ customizable weapons in the SMG, assault, shotgun, sniper, and LMG categories. The game features excellent progression that allows you to unlock new weapons and attachments as you level up. You can fit scopes, skins, keychains, and upgrade the abilities of each gun you own.

Earn more money for gear

Along with the weapons, you can also equip clothing items to improve your base character stats. To get access to these additional items you also need to buy them using the currency you earn from battles. Complete daily and weekly missions to earn even more money to put toward your new gear.

Create custom matches

With the ability to create a custom game, you can also play private matches against friends or more competitive fights against rival clans. The competitive rank system keeps track of the best clans, showing the trophies and XP score to add an extra element of competition.

Play various game modes

Want to jump right into the action? Click quick battle and play a random game on one of the 8 available maps. You can also play a range of other game modes, these are:

  • Team deathmatch
  • Search and destroy
  • Capture the flag
  • Domination
  • Free for all
  • Elimination
  • Gun race

Controls

  • WASD = move
  • Space = jump
  • Left mouse = shoot
  • Right mouse / V = aim
  • P = leaderboard, pause, and settings
  • G = pick up the gun
  • C = crouch
  • Shift = run
  • 1, 2, 3 = switch weapons
  • 4, 5, 6 = skills
  • E = alternative skills
  • Q = alternative skills

FAQ

How do I change my main weapon in Hazmob FPS?

You only have one main weapon until you advance by leveling up and earning coins.

Is Hazmob FPS online only?

Yes, however some of the games include bots.
